What is W&JEMS?

Washington & Jefferson College Emergency Medical Services (W&JEMS) is a licensed Quick Response Service which provides emergency meidcal services, education, and outreach.

Who Are We?

We are a group of student volunteer EMTs (Emergency Medical Technicians) and EMRs (Emergency Medical Responders) who respond to on-campus medical emergencies. Learn More!
